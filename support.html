Support

We’re here to help with account, privacy, and app issues.

Contact

Email: support@bored.app

Typical response time: within 48 hours on business days.

FAQ

  • How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  • How is my data used? See the Privacy Policy for details.
  • Why did I get a repeated activity? We avoid exact repeats for 60 days, but similar categories may appear.
  • Bug report? Email support with screenshots and steps to reproduce.

Privacy questions

For privacy and data deletion requests, email support with the subject line “Privacy Request”.

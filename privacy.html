Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

What we collect

  • Account email and user ID through Firebase Authentication.
  • Activity history and redo usage stored in Firestore.
  • Device metadata required for basic app operation (standard Firebase logs).

How we use data

  • Generate and avoid repeated activity suggestions.
  • Store your history so you can view past activities.
  • Improve reliability and safety of suggestions.

OpenAI usage

Bored? uses OpenAI on our secure servers to generate activity ideas when the curated library is insufficient. We only send recent activity titles to avoid duplicates. We do not send sensitive personal data.

Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time in Settings. Deletion removes your Firebase Auth account, your user profile, and all activity logs.

Your rights

You can request data deletion by emailing support. We respond to requests as quickly as possible, typically within 7 business days.
